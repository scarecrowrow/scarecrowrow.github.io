Scarecrow Row
Armleder Park and Lunken Airport
September 29 - October 14
Who we are Scarecrow Row is a fun, fall art installation being held at Otto Armleder Park, located on the eastside of Cincinnati. Last year was our first year, and we are so happy that Great Parks and Cincinnati Recreation invited us back for a second.

We are looking for artistic individuals, organizations and businesses to create some really cool scarecrows. Read more
